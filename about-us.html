About Us

At Aux TS, we believe your technology should simply work. No fuss, no jargon, no constant headaches. We're here to ensure your IT runs seamlessly.

Kevin Hamm
Co-Founder & CEO

Kevin brings over 7 years of leadership experience as CEO of Auxilyum, Inc., where he ensures technology simply works and stays out of your way so you can focus on getting work done. As a Media Production Director, he specializes in creating targeted training and informational media across multiple platforms.

His expertise spans the entire production pipeline-from film production with casting, writing, and directing to special effects and final distribution. Kevin has managed complete corporate website overhauls, including rewriting back-end services, and excels in web design, testing, and production launch processes.

Beyond Auxilyum, Kevin serves as President of Big Sky Pride, Montana's statewide LGBTQIA+ organization promoting visibility and celebration throughout the state since 2014.

Alex Lee
Co-Founder and CTO

Alex brings a unique blend of creativity and technical expertise to Aux TS. With 25 years of musical experience spanning violin, piano,
guitar, and vocals, he's written and performed extensively while also composing scores for movies and studio projects.
His journey into technology began through sound engineering, which sparked his passion for computers and IT systems.

As President of Auxilyum Technologies since 2022, Alex provides comprehensive cloud services and IT support. He also continues his musical pursuits as a musician/composer with Depth Records, his Helena-based recording studio and production company.

Previously serving as an IT Service Technician for 9 years, Alex specializes in network management, server maintenance, and cybersecurity solutions. When he's not solving tech challenges or
creating music, you'll find him mountain biking through Montana's beautiful terrain.

Dragony McDragon
IT Specialist

Dragony is an IT wizard who can troubleshoot your Wi-Fi while simultaneously reciting the entire works of Shakespeare. With a PhD from MIT (yes, the real one, not the one you found on the internet), Dragony is certified in every technology known to mankind—yes, even that obscure software your uncle swears by.

When he’s not busy saving the world from computer viruses, you can find him in his lair, surrounded by a mountain of tech gadgets, plotting how to steal your girlfriend. Don’t worry, though; he’s not a bad guy—he just has a knack for charming everyone with his dragon-like charisma and a smile that could light up a server room.

In his spare time, Dragony enjoys long walks through the cloud (the digital kind, of course), coding in languages that haven’t even been invented yet, and giving unsolicited advice on how to optimize your life. If you need help with your IT services, just remember: he’s the dragon you want on your side, but maybe keep your girlfriend close!

So, whether you need a tech guru or just someone to make you laugh, Dragony McDragon is your go-to guy. Just don’t ask him to fix your printer—he’s still trying to figure out why it keeps eating paper like it’s a midnight snack!
